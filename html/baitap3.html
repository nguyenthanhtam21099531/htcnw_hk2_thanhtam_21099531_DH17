One particular book which is recommended reading is The Street Lawyer by John Grisham.This book is about a lawyer who begin re-evaluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm. Consequently, he becomes acquainted with the inner city streets, and relizes the harsh existence of the homeless, and vows to give them a chance in the courts. The Street Lawyeris a great book. It is well written and interesting. Others books by John Grisham include The Firm , The Pelican Brief, and The Client
